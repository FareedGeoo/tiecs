About Us

Our Sucsess Story

We started our journey in 2008 by working in graphic designing and for 7 years we worked closely with clients inside and outside Egypt. Since 2015, we have been working in supply chain, logistics and events management with many clients in Egypt such as: Gravity Code and Misr University for Science and Technology.

In 2020 we decided to start our own journey by starting TIECS to provide multiple solution to ease the way you operate your business. We are specialized in supply chain and shipping services as well as production and event management. We will help you achieve your business goals according to a plan that fits your vision. TIECS aims to be your first destination where you can find all your needs in a one place.

Our Vision

To deliver premium service to our clients by meeting their needs under one umbrella which iterates in a fast-paced, agile and efficient cycle.

Our Mission

Building a reliable partnership with our clients by utilizing our resources, team expertise and skillset to help our clients to always be up to date with era.
